<title>Public Information Officer</title>  The Public Information Officer (PIO) is responsible for managing and coordinating all external communications on behalf of CSIS. This includes developing and issuing press releases, responding to media inquiries, preparing official statements, and maintaining the organization’s presence across digital platforms such as the CSIS website and social media. The PIO ensures that all public messaging is accurate, timely, and consistent with CSIS’s mandate, while safeguarding classified or sensitive information.
