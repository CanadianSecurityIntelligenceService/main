Who We Are

The Canadian Security Intelligence Service (CSIS) is Canada’s primary national intelligence service. We are responsible for investigating threats to the security of Canada and safeguarding its interests both domestically and abroad.

Our Mission

To collect, analyze, and report security intelligence to the Government of Canada, helping prevent terrorism, espionage, and foreign interference.
